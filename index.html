The Metric that Matters: Retention

                       

                            My editing philosophy is built for high-tier audiences. Fast cuts, heavy sound effects, and constant motion might spike initial engagement, but they exhaust an educated viewer.                              I edit to enhance the underlying message, structuring pacing to keep the viewer focused on the substance.
